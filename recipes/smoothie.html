Nutty Banana Breakfast Smoothie

Description

Start your day right with a good ol' smoothie. High protein and low calorie. Quick, delicious, and packed with fuel.

Ingredients

  • 1 Frozen Banana
  • 15g Powdered Peanut Butter
  • 1 Scoop of Your Favorite Whey Protein (Vanilla Recommended)
  • 200-250ml milk (Fairlife recommended for added protein and lower sugar content)

Steps

  1. Add ingredients to a blender
  2. Blend until mixture is fully combined in a thick, smooth liquid
  3. Pour into a glass and enjoy!

Nutrition Facts

  • Calories: 320
  • Protein: 31.8g
  • Carbs: 41.8g
  • Fat: 3.6g
